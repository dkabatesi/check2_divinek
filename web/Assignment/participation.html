Bible Scriptures

John chapter 3 verse 16 to 19.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his one and only son,
that whoever believes in him should not perish, but have eternal live.

For God didn’t send his son into the world to judge the world,
but that the world should be saved through him.

He who believes in him is not judged. He who doesn’t believe has been judged already,
because he has not believed in the name of the one and only son of God.

This is the Judgement, that the light has come into the world,
and men loved the darkness rather than light.
